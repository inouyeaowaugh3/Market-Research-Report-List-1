<p><p>The wafer dicing blade market includes two main types: hub dicing blades and hubless dicing blades. Hub dicing blades feature a central hub that provides stability and support during the cutting process, making them ideal for high-volume applications. In contrast, hubless dicing blades lack this central support, resulting in a lighter design that allows for greater maneuverability and precision in intricate cutting tasks. Both types serve the semiconductor industry, offering solutions for efficient wafer separation and processing.</p></p>
