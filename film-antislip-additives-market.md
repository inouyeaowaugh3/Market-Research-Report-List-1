<p><p>The film antislip additives market encompasses various materials used to enhance grip and reduce slip in plastic films. Aluminum silicate and silica are two primary types. Aluminum silicate offers durability and high abrasion resistance, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. Silica, known for its versatility, provides excellent transparency and is effective in preventing slipping in various conditions. Both additives improve safety and performance in products like packaging films, floor coverings, and automotive applications by enhancing friction and enhancing surface properties.</p></p>
