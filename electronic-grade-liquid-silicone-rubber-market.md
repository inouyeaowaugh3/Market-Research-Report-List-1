<p><p>The Electronic Grade Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) market is primarily divided into two types: Single Component LSR and Two Component LSR. Single Component LSR is pre-mixed and ready to use, offering convenience and consistency, suitable for applications requiring quick curing. In contrast, Two Component LSR involves a polymer and a curing agent, which must be mixed before use, providing flexibility in formulation and enhancing properties. Both types are essential for various electronic applications, including encapsulation and insulation.</p></p>
