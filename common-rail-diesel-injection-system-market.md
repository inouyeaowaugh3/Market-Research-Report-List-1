<p><p>The Common Rail Diesel Injection System market features two primary types: Hot Forged Rail and Laser Welding Rail. Hot Forged Rail is constructed through high-temperature forging processes, ensuring durability and robustness, which enhances injection precision and efficiency. In contrast, Laser Welding Rail employs advanced laser technology for precise welds, allowing for intricate designs and improved fuel atomization. Both types contribute to enhanced engine performance, reduced emissions, and increased fuel efficiency, catering to the evolving demands of the automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
