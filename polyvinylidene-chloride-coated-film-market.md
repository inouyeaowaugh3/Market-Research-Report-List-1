<p><ul><li>Coated PVDC One Side</li><li>Coated PVDC Two Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyvinylidene Chloride (PVDC) coated film market is divided into two primary types: coated PVDC one side and coated PVDC two side. Coated PVDC one side features a coating applied to only one surface, making it suitable for applications that require moisture and gas barrier properties on a single side, such as food packaging. In contrast, coated PVDC two side offers enhanced barrier protection on both surfaces, ideal for applications demanding high durability and preservation, such as pharmaceuticals and sensitive electronics.</p></p>
