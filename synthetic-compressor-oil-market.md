<p><p>The synthetic compressor oil market is primarily categorized into three types: PAO (Polyalphaolefins), PAG (Polyalkylene Glycols), and Esters. PAO is known for its excellent thermal stability and low volatility, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. PAG offers superior lubrication and is often water-soluble, enhancing efficiency in certain systems. Esters, derived from natural or synthetic sources, provide excellent lubrication and biodegradability, making them environmentally friendly. Each type caters to specific needs in compressor performance and longevity, contributing to the market's diversity.</p></p>
