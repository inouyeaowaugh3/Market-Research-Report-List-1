<p><p>The non-metallic industrial gasket market comprises various types designed for specific sealing applications. Spiral wound gaskets are made from alternating layers of metal and non-metal materials, providing high flexibility and resilience. Ring joint gaskets are typically used in high-pressure applications, featuring a circular shape that ensures a tight seal. Corrugated gaskets consist of a wavy structure that enhances compressibility and sealing performance. Other types may include foam, rubber, and composite gaskets tailored for diverse industrial environments and chemical compatibility.</p></p>
