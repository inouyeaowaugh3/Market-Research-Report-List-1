<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) insurance market is divided into two primary types: compulsory insurance and optional insurance. Compulsory insurance is mandated by law, typically covering third-party liabilities to protect against damages or injuries caused by NEVs. Optional insurance, however, is not legally required and includes coverage like comprehensive and collision insurance, allowing owners to protect their vehicles against theft, damage, or accidents. Both types play crucial roles in promoting NEV adoption and ensuring financial security for vehicle owners.</p></p>
