<p><ul><li>Soda-lime</li><li>Synthetic Quartz</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TFT-LCD photomask market includes two primary types: soda-lime and synthetic quartz. Soda-lime photomasks are cost-effective and commonly used for mass production, offering suitable transparency and durability for various applications. Synthetic quartz, on the other hand, provides superior optical performance and chemical resistance, making it ideal for high-precision tasks in advanced display manufacturing. Both types play crucial roles in the photolithography process, influencing the performance and quality of TFT-LCD panels in the electronics industry.</p></p>
