<p><p>The residential fitting market comprises two primary types: metal pipe fittings and plastic pipe fittings. Metal pipe fittings, often made from materials like brass or stainless steel, are known for their durability and resistance to high temperatures and pressures, making them suitable for plumbing and heating systems. In contrast, plastic pipe fittings, typically made from PVC or CPVC, are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and easier to install, making them ideal for residential water supply and drainage systems. Each type offers distinct advantages based on specific application needs.</p></p>
