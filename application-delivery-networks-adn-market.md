<p><strong>The Application Delivery Networks (ADN) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Application Delivery Controllers (ADC)</li><li>WAN Optimization Controllers (WOC)</li><li>Application Security Equipment</li><li>Application Gateways</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Application Delivery Networks (ADN) optimize and secure the delivery of applications over networks. </p><p>1. **Application Delivery Controllers (ADC)** manage traffic, ensuring availability and performance.</p><p>2. **WAN Optimization Controllers (WOC)** enhance data transfer across Wide Area Networks, reducing latency and increasing throughput.</p><p>3. **Application Security Equipment** safeguards applications from threats, ensuring data integrity and confidentiality.</p><p>4. **Application Gateways** act as intermediaries between users and applications, facilitating secure communication and controlling access. </p><p>Together, these components enhance application performance and security.</p></p>
