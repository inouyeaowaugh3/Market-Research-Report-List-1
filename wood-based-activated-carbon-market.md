<p><p>The wood-based activated carbon market includes three main types: powdered activated carbon (PAC), granular activated carbon (GAC), and others. PAC is finely powdered and used for applications requiring rapid adsorption, such as water treatment. GAC consists of larger granules, suitable for air and water purification, providing a longer contact time for efficient adsorption. "Others" may encompass specialized forms like extruded or shaped activated carbon, tailored for unique industrial applications, enhancing overall versatility in environmental and industrial processes.</p></p>
