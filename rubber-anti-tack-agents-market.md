<p><p>The rubber anti-tack agents market comprises various types including stearates, fatty acid esters, fatty acid amides, and soaps. Stearates, often derived from fatty acids, reduce stickiness during processing. Fatty acid esters act as lubricants and improve flow properties. Fatty acid amides enhance thermal stability and reduce friction. Soaps, typically alkaline compounds, provide a barrier reducing adhesion. These agents are essential in manufacturing rubber products, facilitating handling and enhancing overall performance in various applications.</p></p>
