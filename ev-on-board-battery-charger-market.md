<p><strong>The EV On Board Battery Charger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unidirectional</li><li>Bidirectional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EV on-board battery charger market can be categorized into unidirectional and bidirectional charging systems. Unidirectional chargers allow energy to flow only from the power source to the electric vehicle (EV) battery, facilitating charging but not energy return. In contrast, bidirectional chargers enable two-way energy flow, allowing the EV to not only charge itself but also discharge energy back to the grid or power home appliances. This dual functionality enhances energy management and supports grid stability during peak demand.</p></p>
